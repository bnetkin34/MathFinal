QUOTIENT RULE

Learn how to take a derivative and apply that knowledge using these methods!

Definition of the Quotient Rule: A method used to differeniate two functions being divided by one another.

Function Notation: d/dx(f(x)/g(x)) = ((g(x)*df(x)) - (f(x)*dg(x)))/(g(x))2

Steps to Success:

Step 1: Identify f(x) and g(x) separately and find their derivatives.
Step 2: Plug everything into the formula shown above.
Step 3: Simplify, if needed.
